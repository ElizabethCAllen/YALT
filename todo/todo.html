What Not To Do During an Interview

Interviews, we get it, they are scary! However, feeling and being prepared might make an interview less intimidating and give you a better chance of being successful. With that being said, an interview may also quickly go south if you make silly common mistakes. Don't worry, we got you covered.

  1. You're Late
      If you are late to an interview then it shows that you will be late for the job. Be On time!
  2. Lying
      Just don't do it. If your interviewer catches you in the lie, there is a guarantee you will not get hired
  3. Texting or answering a call
      Make sure your phone is on silent and do not...DO NOT answer a text or a phone call during the interview. If shows you are uninterested in the job opportunity and have no respect for the interviwer.
  4. Arrogance
      You might be highly qualified for the job, and know alot for the position but no not be a know it all! There is always room to learn in any position.
  5. Dressing inappropriately
      Need tips on what to wear for an interview? Visit our "How to Dress page"
  6. Complaining
      Lack of accountability is not something a hiring manager is looking for. Do not blame others for your mistakes. Instead talk about how you overcame mistakes.
  7. Not Making Eye Contact
      By not looking someone in the eye, this may appear as dishonesty or lack of trust.
  8. Refusing to smile
      By not smiling, this may appear as you being cold or standoffish.
  9. Fidgeting too much
      We are not telling you to be as stiff as a rock, but it's distracting if you are constantly moving.
  10. Not Thanking the interviewer
      It leaves a lasting impression if you share your appreciation towards the end of the interview.

You will get the job! And if you didn't there is always the next one. Each interview will become easier, and soon enough you will land your dream job! And when that happens you can give us a shout out for helping you! There is only one you, you are amazing!
