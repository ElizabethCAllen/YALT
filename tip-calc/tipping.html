Tip Calculator

How to calculate a tip? Choosing a tip percent or a dollar amount can sometimes be a little puzzling. If you want to find out how to calculate a tip, you need to decide how much you appreciate the service. There is an unwritten rule that a tip should be between 10% and 20% of the total value of the bill. It may be hard to imagine somebody sitting at the restaurant table and calculating the percentage of the bill, but there is a relatively easy way to do it. When you want to count 10% of your bill, it's quite simple.

Your bill is $150.00

1st- Enter $150.00 under the amount box.

2nd- Enter the percent you want to tip under Tip or enter a flat dollar amount under Tip amount.

3rd- If you selected a percentage, Click the "Calculate tip from Rate button" . If you selected a flat dollar amount, Click the "Calculate tip% from tip $ button".
