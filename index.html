YALT - Young Adult Life Tips

Young Adult Life Tips will teach users some life skills that are not typically taught in an education setting. This application will allow users to browse different tips and advice to help the user apply it to their certain needs.

Banking Category includes: how to write a check, how to open up a bank account, how to apply for a credit card, etc.

Job Interview Category includes: how to dress, questions to ask them, resume, etc.

Automotive Category includes: changing a flat tire, where to go get an oil change, jump start your car etc.

Life Skills Category includes: cooking, cleaning, laundry, change a light bulb, etc.
