How to Dress For Interviews

Before you say a single word to the interviewer, you have already made an impression based on what you are wearing! The tips given here are commonly accepted as appropriate for interviewing. Every company or organization has a different dress code or work enviroment; the suitable dress code will depend on the job you are interviewing for! And remember, the most important thing to wear for an interview is a big, happy smile!

      Masculine Attire
  1. Wear a Suit
      Matching solid color jacket and pants
      Tie
      Long sleve shirt that coordinates with suit jacket
      Dark Belt
      Matching socks
  2. Avoid bright colors and cartoonish ties
  3. Clothing should be clean, ironed, neat, and properly fitted
  4. Wear subtle cologne or fragrance spray
  5. Hair should be neat, clean, and conservative
  6. Make sure to have fresh breath. Do not chew gum during the interview
