How To Dress for an Interview

Before you say a single word to the interviewer, you have already made an impression based on what you are wearing! The tips given here are commonly accepted as appropriate for interviewing. Every company or organization has a different dress code or work environment; the suitable dress code will depend on the job you are interviewing for. And remember the most important thing to wear for an interview is a big, bright smile with confidence!

      Feminine Attire
  1. Matching Suit Pants or Skirt
  2. Clothing should be neat, fitted, ironed, and clean
  3. Solid Color Suit is prefered
  4. Solid color blouse that matches the suit
  5. Closed toe footwear-avoie open toed footwear
  6. Hair should be combed and not covering the face
  7. Make up and nail polish should be clean and flattering but not too flashy
  8. Fresh Breath-Do not chew gum during interview

      Masculine Attire
  1. Matching Suit Jacket and Pants
  2. Clothing should be neat, fitted, ironed, and clean
  3. Solid Color Tie
  4. Matching Socks
  5. Hair should be combed and neat
  6. Stay away from bright colors
  7. Fresh Breath-Do not chew gum during interview
