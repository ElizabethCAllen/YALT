How to Check Tire Tread

There is a simple way to check to see if you need new tires: the penny test. All you need is a penny, old or new, it doesn’t matter. Take your penny and put the top of Lincoln’s head into the tread of the tire.

If Lincoln’s head is always covered by the tread of the tire, you don’t need new tires. But, if you can see Lincoln’s head, this means your tires are worn and they don’t have a lot of tread on them, meaning your tires need to be replaced soon.

Why should you worry about tire tread? When your tire treads are worn, your car might not respond in the best way in bad conditions like rain and snow. If you have a good tire tread, your car will grip the road better. And finally, worn treads can make other parts of your car wear prematurely.
